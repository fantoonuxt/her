Our Story

It was in Ancient Egypt, a civilization renowned for its opulence and mystique, that cosmetics were first created. Pharaohs, being pioneers in the art of beauty, left a mark in history; when one thinks of Pharaoh Queens like Nefertiti and Cleopatra, the imagery of their adorned, vibrant makeup is automatically conjured, with lipstick playing an especially pivotal role in their beauty rituals.

This rich heritage is what inspired Her Nefer to emerge; Her means face, and Nefer means beauty in our ancient Pharaonic language.

We are choosing to channel the timeless allure of Ancient Egypt into modern, luxurious cosmetics. By blending tradition with innovation, Her Nefer offers a touch of ancient royal elegance to the women of today, redefining the concept of Egyptian beauty for a new era.

Leave Your Mark
